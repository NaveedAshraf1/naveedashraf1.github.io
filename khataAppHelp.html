Your questions, answered.

Getting Started

How do I sign in?
You can sign in using your Google account. This helps keep your data synced and secure.

Is the app free?
Yes, Khata Register is completely free to use.

Using the App

How do I add a customer?
Tap the + Add Customer button and enter details like name, phone, balance, and optionally an image.

How do I record a payment?
Select a customer, then tap Add Payment or Add Sale to update their balance.

Where is my data stored?
All your data is securely stored in Firebase cloud services linked to your Google account.

Account & Data

Can I delete my account?
Yes. Go to the app settings and select Delete Account. This will remove all your data permanently.

Can I backup my data?
Your data is automatically backed up in Firebase. You don’t need to do anything manually.

Troubleshooting

App not syncing?
Make sure you are connected to the internet. Try signing out and signing back in to refresh your session.

App crashed?
Don’t worry! We use Firebase Crashlytics to monitor and fix issues quickly. You can also send us feedback via email.
