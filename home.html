Welcome to the Attendance Register Help Center! We're here to assist you with any questions or issues you may have.

👇 Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I mark attendance?
Select a student and tap on the date to choose their attendance status: Present, Absent, or Leave.
2. Can I view attendance history?
Yes! Go to the “Reports” section to view monthly or daily attendance for each student.
3. How do I add a new class or student?
Use the “+” button in the Classes or Students section to add new entries.
4. What if I made a mistake?
Simply tap the date again and update the attendance status.
5. Is my data safe?
Yes. All your data is stored securely in Firebase with your Google account.
